👩‍⚕️About Hypoxic Burden

Hypoxic Burden (HB) quantifies the cumulative oxygen desaturation load during sleep by integrating both the depth and duration of SpO₂ drops. Unlike simple event counts (AHI), HB captures the actual physiological stress of intermittent hypoxia on cardiovascular and neural systems.

Calculation: HB = Σ (desaturation area below baseline) / total sleep hours → unit: %·min/hr

This implementation follows the methodology by Sutherland et al. (Sleep, 2022), validated across multiple sleep cohorts. HB has proven to be a stronger predictor of cardiovascular outcomes than traditional metrics, as demonstrated by Azarbarzin et al. (AJRCCM, 2019). We provide this tool for research use, but do not guarantee regulatory compliance.
